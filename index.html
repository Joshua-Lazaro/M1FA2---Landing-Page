What is FreshSense?

FreshSense is an innovative air quality monitoring system designed specifically for mushroom farms. By detecting early signs of contamination through odor analysis, it helps farmers maintain optimal growing conditions and protect their valuable crops.

Why FreshSense?

Smart Odor Sensor

This small device is placed inside mushroom growing rooms. It checks the air for certain smells that are known to be early signs of trouble. It doesn’t need water, sunlight, or complicated setup— just place it and turn it on.

Mobile App for Farmers

The app shows what FreshSense is detecting. If the air is clean and healthy, everything appears green. If something unusual starts to happen, the app changes color and sends a clear message:

How It Works

FreshSense continuously “smells” the air inside mushroom farms. By analyzing odor patterns, it helps growers detect potential risks early—ensuring healthier crops, reduced losses, and peace of mind.
